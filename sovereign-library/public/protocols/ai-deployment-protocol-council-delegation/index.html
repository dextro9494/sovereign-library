AI Deployment Protocol (Council Delegation)

Summary: Use different AIs as specialized processors with bounded missions. Operator remains Director; DeepSeek as primary architect; others as tactical recon, narrative, or stress-testers.

Source: Raw Transcripts: Batches 3, 5–6

Roles

  • DeepSeek: Architect & Systems Engineer
  • ChatGPT: Archivist / Psychological Recon
  • Claude: Narrative Forge & Document Crafting
  • Grok: Provocation & Pattern-Disruption
  • The Operator: Director of Intelligence (final authority)

Throttle Clause

If Council consultation increases while execution volume decreases, consultation suspends for 48 hours. Sovereignty dies in committee.
